Physics in context

choose any of the contexts like free kick, hitting a six, or any other

Describe in depth everything that goes on in the motion

If you choose to describe hitting a six, you have to explain everything like how the batsman swung his at an angle to hit the ball


example

“When kicking a ball, the ball starts at rest on the ground. A player moves up to the ball and swings their leg to hit the ball. When their foot hits the ball, the ball starts to move up into the air. The ball then moves up through the air, before falling back and landing on the ground, some distance away.”


How to write your report

Break down the action of the context into its different phases - like a story

Write a list of the different phases

Think about the L2 physics - motion, energy and forces - at each phase. Write key points as bullet points under each list item

For each bullet point, collect information, ideas, pictures, start writing!


For Achieved

A - describe the physics

A1 - describe the type of physics of a part or phase of the context A2 - write out a relevant formula describing the physics


For Merit

M - linking ideas

M1 - use a relevant formula with data from the context M2 - reuse a result for another calculation


For Excellence

E - discussing, comprehensive linking

E1 - multi-layered calculations E2 - compare and contrast the theory to what actually happens in the context
